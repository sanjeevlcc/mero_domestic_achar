Our Story

Himalayan Achar was started with one simple idea: to bring the traditional taste of Nepali homemade pickle to every table. Our recipes are inspired by the flavors found in village kitchens, family gatherings, and everyday dal bhat meals.

We prepare achar in small batches to maintain freshness, taste, and quality. Every jar is made with selected ingredients, carefully mixed spices, and hygienic packing.

✅ Made with fresh local ingredients
✅ Traditional Nepali masala and mustard oil
✅ Hygienic jar packing and careful storage

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Simple answers for common customer questions.

How long can achar be stored?

If stored properly in a clean, dry place and handled with a dry spoon, achar can last for several weeks to months depending on the variety.

Is delivery available?

Yes, delivery is available. You can add your address in the order form or contact us directly by phone or WhatsApp.

Yes, you can mention your preferred spice level in the message box while placing your order.

Yes, we provide bulk and regular supply options for restaurants, cafes, hotels, and grocery stores.
